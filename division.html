Types of Sectors

STPI

STPI was established in 1991 by the Indian Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY) with the objective of encouraging, promoting and boosting the export of software from India. STPI Headquarters is located in New Delhi with over 60+ Centres spread across the country.

COE

National Centre of Excellence for Cyber Security Technology Development and Product Entrepreneurship is a joint initiative of the Data Security Council of India (DSCI) and the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY) that works as a multiplier for cybersecurity research, innovation, and product development.

NeGD

n 2009, National e-Governance Division was created by the Ministry of Electronics & Information Technology as an Independent Business Division under the Digital India Corporation {erstwhile Media Lab Asia}. Since 2009, NeGD has been playing a pivotal role in supporting MeitY in Programme Management and implementation of the e-Governance Projects
